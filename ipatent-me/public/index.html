💡 Claims Best Practices:
• Independent claims (standalone) define broad protection. Start with "A method…" "An apparatus…" "A system…" or "A composition…"
• Dependent claims add specificity: "The method of claim 1, wherein…"
• Use "comprising" (open-ended, preferred) rather than "consisting of" (closed)
• Each claim is ONE sentence. Use semicolons and line breaks for readability.
• Claims are NOT required in a provisional application, but including them strengthens your priority date per 35 USC §119(e).
