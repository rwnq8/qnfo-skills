## Findings Summary

- **"Harmonic" is place-dependent.** The Vladimirov p-adic oscillator is harmonic in the p-adic sense (equal p-adic distances) but lacks the ladder algebra and IR attractor properties of the Archimedean HO.
- **The IR attractor is ∞-place-specific.** The β-function is defined via d/d(ln μ) with μ ∈ ℝ. No p-adic β-function has been constructed.
- **RS-1 α⁻¹ ≈ 137 is cosmetic.** The rational core satisfies the adelic product formula trivially (as every rational does). ε ≈ 0.036 has no p-adic interpretation.
- **The HP was an Archimedean theory.** Non-Archimedean bibliography was context-setting, not completion-theoretic reasoning.
