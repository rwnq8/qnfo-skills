## Stage 8: Self-Critique

1. **CRITICAL: Material science bottleneck.** The entire cascade depends on FCI material synthesis at scale — a field that has delivered zero room-temperature topological qubits to date. If FCI fabrication remains stuck at mK temperatures for another 20 years, Eras 1-4 collapse.
2. **SIGNIFICANT: LLM inference gap.** The hydrodynamic quantum foundations argument (Era 3) relies on mathematical claims that require formal proof verification — an LLM cannot validate these. Flag for human expert review.
3. **SIGNIFICANT: Institutional inertia.** Even if Era 3 succeeds mathematically, academic adoption of a "quantum mechanics is wrong" framework faces 30-50 years of resistance (historical precedent: plate tectonics took 50 years from Wegener to acceptance).
4. **MINOR: Prior calibration.** The 0.25/0.60/0.15 priors for Era 1 are heuristic — no formal elicitation process was used. Sensitivity analysis shows the top-3 portfolio is robust to ±20% prior variation.

**When to ignore this critique:** If FCI material breakthroughs accelerate (e.g., twisted bilayer graphene results show unexpected topological phases), the material science bottleneck dissolves and the cascade probabilities tighten significantly.
