## Zitterbewegung Supplement — The Compton Helix

A particle is not a point mass but a dimension that "coils" from linear/Archimedean motion in physical 3D+1 space to helical motion at the Compton scale. Time is fundamentally Compton frequency — the rate at which the helical structure completes one coil. The Foldy-Wouthuysen transformation in QFT, which "removes" Zitterbewegung to produce a point-particle representation, is reinterpreted as a deliberate geometric simplification that discards the helical structure [speculative, _26195165402.md].

**Implication for Era 3 (Hydrodynamic QM):** If particles are helical structures rather than points, the Madelung hydrodynamic reformulation gains a concrete geometric interpretation: the "probability fluid" is the actual Compton-scale helical motion, and "measurement" is the projection of this 4D helix onto 3D space at a specific Compton phase. This bridges the mathematical Madelung transform to a physical interpretation.

**Implication for Era 4 (Pre-Geometric):** The Compton helix is a scale-invariant geometric primitive — if all particles are helical coils of varying winding number on the S¹ manifold, then mass ratios and coupling constants emerge naturally as ratios of winding numbers (π and φ combinations), eliminating the need for empirical parameter fitting.
