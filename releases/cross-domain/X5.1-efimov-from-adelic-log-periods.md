In 1970, Vitaly Efimov predicted that three identical bosons with resonant pairwise interactions would form an infinite tower of bound states whose binding energies form a geometric progression:

$$E_n = E_0 \cdot e^{-2\pi n / s_0}$$

where s₀ ≈ 1.00624 is the solution to the transcendental equation:

The Efimov effect was experimentally confirmed in 2006 using ultracold cesium atoms (Kraemer et al., *Nature* 440, 315), making it one of the few experimentally verified RG limit cycles in nature.

The log-period at place p is ln(p). At each p-adic place, the RG flow is a discrete multiplicative cascade with fundamental step p.
